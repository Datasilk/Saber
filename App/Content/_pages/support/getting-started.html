Getting Started with Gmaster

So you want to create email campaigns within Gmail. That's awesome! Now let's get you started on your journey to engaging with your potential customers!

Get the Chrome Extension

Gmaster is built to load within Gmail, so before you can begin using it, you must first install the Gmaster Chrome extension. In order to install the extension, you'll first need to download the Chrome web browser and open this website via Chrome.

After opening Chrome, you can click the button at the top-right corner of this page labeled "Install Chrome Extension". From within the Chrome Web Store, you'll be able to install the Gmaster Chrome Extension. Once you're done, come back to this page and follow the next steps.

Sign In to Gmail

To sign up for Gmaster or log into your existing Gmaster account, you will be prompted with a Google Sign-In popup window once you go to www.gmail.com and log into your Gmail account. Once you sign into your Google account using the account associated with your Gmail inbox, your email address will be automatically registered with Gmaster. Afterwards, you won't be prompted for the Google Sign-In window again unless you log in to Gmail from another PC, or from another Google account in Chrome.

Choose a Subscription

Once you've logged into your Gmaster account within Gmail, if you don't already have a subscription plan, you'll automatically be redirected to the subscription plan page. From there, you can choose from four plan types; Beginner, Daily, Pro, & Team plans.

Each plan will have unique benefits based on price, and the Team plan will have all the benefits of the Pro plan, but at a lower price per user (minimum 2 users).

Pay with Card or PayPal

After choosing a subscription, you'll be given the choice of paying with either a credit card or with PayPal. Once you decide your payment method, it may take a few moments before your payment is approved, and then all of Gmaster's features will be unlocked within Gmail.

Credit Card

All payments are processed using Stripe and you'll see the an item labeled "Gmaster for Gmail" on your credit card statement.

PayPal

In order to pay with PayPal, you'll need to have an active PayPal account and you'll be prompted to log into your account upon selecting your payment method.
