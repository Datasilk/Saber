Gmaster Features within Gmail

  • Email Campaigns
    Create personalized email campaigns and schedule them to be sent out to your customers.
  • Auto-Followup Emails
    Configure your campaigns to send auto-followup emails based on how your customers respond.
  • Address Book
    Collect data about your potential customers to further personalize your email campaigns.
  • Google Sheets
    Import Google Sheets data to build a list of customers within your address book.
  • Personalized Emails
    Inject personalized content into your emails based on the data collected within your address book
  • Surveys
    Engage with your customers using a set of questions that they can answer within their emails.
  • Unlimited Emails
    Send personalized emails to your customers while bypassing your Gmail daily sending limits.
  • 3rd Party Emailing
    Send even more emails per day using SendGrid or other SMTP service providers.
  • Drafts
    Test your campaigns by generating draft emails instead of sending emails to your customers.
  • Support
    Read documentation from within Gmail & get online support from the Gmaster community
